Introducing Form 1A - GCTS

The Individual Inventory Record Form meticulously crafted by the Guidance, Counseling, and Testing Services of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. This comprehensive form serves as a pivotal tool in understanding and supporting each student's unique journey. By capturing vital personal, educational, and familial details, it empowers educators and counselors to provide tailored guidance and assistance. Through its structured layout, the form facilitates efficient data collection, ensuring accuracy and completeness. Furthermore, the utmost attention is paid to data privacy, with stringent measures in place to safeguard students' sensitive information. Rest assured, all inputs are treated with the highest confidentiality, fostering a safe and trusting environment for students to express themselves. This form not only benefits the school in enhancing its support services but also ensures that students feel valued and respected, knowing that their privacy is prioritized. With Form 1A - GCTS, students can embark on their academic journey with confidence, knowing that their individual needs and concerns are understood and addressed effectively.
